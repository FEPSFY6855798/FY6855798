Abstract

        This article is an insight into a range of developments in sustainable aviation, focusing on sustainable aviation fuels and briefly looking at
        electric-powered aircraft. Taking research from a range of books, articles, and projects, I will present to you both promising and innovative
        developments which are being used to both decarbonise aviation and increase its efficiency and affordability for people across the globe. 
        The aerospace Industry is currently one of the most dynamic and growing industries in engineering and this article aims to both educate and
        encourage readers to play their part in this worldwide fight against global warming.

Introduction to Sustainable Engineering

        Aviation contributed to around 2% of carbon emissions in 2022. (European Union, 2023). Despite this, I personally believe that innovation is at
        an all-time high and that this dynamic industry will continue to develop and grow in efficiency as well as sustainability. I have no doubt that
        engineers will continue to innovate and help mitigate the potentially drastic impacts of global warming and climate change.
